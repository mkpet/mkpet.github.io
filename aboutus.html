About us

We started MK Pet Shop in April of 2009,which was a shop that only sells dog and dog food. At that time, the store does not have cats and cat foods for sale. We decided to expand our company to the next level not just for the profits,but also bring joy and happiness to the pet owner.
In a world which is becoming more impersonal, hurried and stressful, we provide our customers with a fun place to shop, where you will find a great selection of pet products at competitive prices and friendly, helpful staff to take care of you. We're big enough to carry a wide selection of pet products, but not so big that you can't find what you need. At MK Pet Shop, you won't get lost in the crowd and you won't get an automated answering system when you call us. During store hours, you always talk to a "live person" every time you call.
We also believe that MK Pet Shop has a responsibility to our community. Because we are sad to see how many unwanted and abandoned dogs and cats are euthanized every day, we choose not to ignore the puppies and kittens. We encourage our customers to buy the cats and dogs from us or any pet shop to give them love and care. Come and see us at MK Pet Shop, and find the best supplies you can get for your newest family member”.
We hope you enjoy our website, and that you visit often. If you haven't stopped by our store yet, we invite you to come in and say hello to us. Oh, and don't be surprised to find both of our CEOs in the shop.
