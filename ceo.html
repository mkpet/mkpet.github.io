A Message Bought To You By Our CEO

  Recently, we've paid a visit to the SPCA animal shelter, we saw a lot of lovely yet pitiful puppies and kittens. They are so adorable when they are playing with each other, but it is rather heartbreaking to see them living without a proper shelter and an official owner.

We hope to let the society know that cats and dogs are living thing too, they deserve to be loved and cared just like how we treat our family and friends. If human needs a house as a shelter, then the animals would be the same.

  We also hope to create a project named Tne home one pet to emphasize on the society telling that everyone should give the pet a shelter and everyone can all adopt or buy a pet. If this project was to spread over the world, we believe, one day, there will be zero stray dogs or stray cats on the street or being executed.
