Our vision

Our vision is to be the leader in the animal industry. Not only that, we want to maintain and further develop our presence and strength in the area of pet caring and pet supplies. An international network of suppliers are what we continuously strive for.
Besides that, we hope that MK Pet Shop can expand to the level that we are able to work together with the Animal Medical Centre to learn their exceptional service, technological superiority and so on. We also hope to give health and care service to pet, not only selling them. Last but not least, we hope to expand to the level where we can give full service as in health and care, grooming, supplies and so on.
